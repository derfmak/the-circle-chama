About THE CIRCLE

Who We Are

THE CIRCLE is a registered chama (investment group) established to promote financial discipline and collective wealth creation among our members. We believe in the power of regular contributions, prudent investments, and mutual support.

How It Works

1

Monthly Contributions

Members contribute KES 1,000 monthly by the 10th of each month.

2

Quarterly Payments

Additional KES 7,000 quarterly for major investments.

3

Event Support

Quarterly events where one member receives full support.

4

Meetings

Regular meetings to discuss progress and plan ahead.

Membership Benefits

  • Structured savings discipline
  • Access to emergency funds
  • Investment opportunities
  • Networking and community support
  • Transparent financial management
